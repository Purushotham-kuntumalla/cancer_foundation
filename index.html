types of cancer

Lung cancer is a type of cancer that starts in the lungs, usually in the cells lining the air passages. It is one of the most common types of cancer and a leading cause of cancer-related deaths worldwide. Lung cancer is often associated with smoking, but non-smokers can also develop the disease

Breast cancer is a type of cancer that forms in the cells of the breast. It is the most common cancer among women worldwide, although men can also develop it, albeit at a lower rate. Breast cancer typically originates in the milk ducts or lobules of the breast.

Stomach cancer, also known as gastric cancer, is a type of cancer that develops in the cells of the stomach lining. It is a relatively common cancer and can affect people of all ages, although it is more prevalent in older individuals. Stomach cancer can have a significant impact on a person's health and well-being
